⚖️ Regulations & City Hall SF · Oakland · CA
🔴 SF Rent Cap: 1.4% through Feb 28, 2026

San Francisco Rent Board effective March 1, 2025. Pre-1979 buildings only. Must obtain rent increase license before any increase.

🟡 Oakland RAP Cap: ~2.7%

Oakland Rent Adjustment Program. Buildings pre-1983. CPI-tied. Must file with RAP before any increase on covered units (94609).

🟢 AB 1482 Statewide: 5% + CPI, max 10%

Applies to buildings built before Jan 1, 2005, not covered by stricter local ordinance. Newer SF/Oakland units may fall here.

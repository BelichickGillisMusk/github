I-5 & Highway 99 Corridors

We serve the full Sacramento-to-Merced stretch along I-5 and Highway 99, including truck stops, rest areas, and distribution centers. Perfect for long-haul operators who need testing along their route.

Major Highway Coverage

  • I-5: Sacramento to Los Banos, including West Sacramento, Lodi, Stockton, Tracy, Patterson, Newman
  • Highway 99: Sacramento to Merced, including Elk Grove, Galt, Lodi, Stockton, Manteca, Modesto, Turlock, Atwater

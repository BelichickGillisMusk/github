What to Include When You Contact Us

To schedule testing or get a quote, please provide:

  • Your location: City or street address where you want testing
  • Vehicle info: Year, make, model, and GVWR (if known)
  • Number of vehicles: Single vehicle or fleet size
  • Preferred date/time: Any day/time window that works for you
  • Test type (if known): OBD, OVI-J1667, or not sure

We'll confirm your appointment, provide pricing, and answer any questions.

### 🥇 Tier 1 — Do these first (biggest impact, all free)

#### 1. Bing Places for Business
- **URL:** https://www.bingplaces.com
- **Why:** Powers Bing, Yahoo Local, DuckDuckGo, AOL. Imports automatically from Google Business Profile in 2 clicks.
- **Time:** ~5 min
- **Steps:**
  1. Sign in with the same email used for Google Business Profile (or any Microsoft account)
  2. Choose **"Import from Google Business Profile"**
  3. Authorize → review → publish
- **Status:** ☐ Not claimed

#### 2. Apple Business Connect
- **URL:** https://businessconnect.apple.com
- **Why:** Apple Maps, Siri, Spotlight, iMessage business search. **Critical for any local attorney** — every iPhone user who searches "family lawyer near me" sees this.
- **Time:** ~10 min
- **Steps:**
  1. Sign in with Apple ID
  2. Create a Business Connect profile (free)
  3. Add the location → claim **4815 Laguna Park Dr, Ste C, Elk Grove, CA 95758**
  4. Verify by phone (they call (916) 230-6381 with a code)
  5. Add categories (Family Law Attorney, Immigration Attorney)
  6. Add hours, photos, description (use the medium description above)
- **Status:** ☐ Not claimed

#### 3. Avvo
- **URL:** https://www.avvo.com
- **Why:** #1 attorney-specific directory. People literally search "find a lawyer" → Avvo. The Avvo Rating shows up in Google. **Most important attorney directory by far.**
- **Time:** ~15 min (they may already auto-list him from State Bar data — search his name first)
- **Steps:**
  1. Search "Clifford Chigbu Elk Grove" on avvo.com — likely already listed
  2. Click **"Claim your free profile"** at the bottom of his existing page (or sign up)
  3. Verify with State Bar # 221386
  4. Fill in: practice areas, languages, fees, education, photo
  5. **Important:** Add at least 1 case story per practice area — Avvo's algorithm favors profiles with content
- **Status:** ☐ Not claimed

#### 4. Justia Lawyer Directory

- **Why:** Free, automatically pulls from State Bar data. Strong SEO citation. Justia profiles regularly rank on page 1 of Google for attorney name searches.

## 🚨 Things to watch out for

1. **Don't pay for premium tiers.** Free listings get 95% of the SEO benefit. Paid tiers rarely return their cost for solo attorneys.
2. **Use the same NAP every time.** "(916) 230-6381" not "916-230-6381" or "+1 916 230 6381". Pick one format and use it everywhere.
3. **Refuse to upsell.** Many directories will pressure you with "premium" trials. Always say no.
4. **Phone-verify when offered.** It's the strongest ownership signal and prevents competitors from hijacking the listing.
5. **Don't create duplicate listings.** Always search for an existing listing first and claim it instead of creating a new one.
6. **Use the desk photo for everywhere.** Once we have the photo, upload the same one to every directory — visual consistency matters.
